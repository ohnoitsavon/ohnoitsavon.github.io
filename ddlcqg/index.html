Hi, welcome to the DDLC Quote Generator Doki Bots - created by ohnoitsAvon

What are the DDLC Quote Generator (DDLCQG) Doki Bots, you ask? DDLCQG Doki Bots are a set of Discord bots that allows you to interact with your favourite characters from DDLC.

This project started life as a quote generator but has since evolved into a fully interactive experience with multiple bots. You can do everything from giving the dokis a hug to playing a version of the poem minigame.

Below is a list of current features:

  • Actions: Hug, headpat, tickle, or boop a Doki.
  • Ask: Ask a Doki a yes or no question.
  • Attack: Let your inner yandere run wild.
  • Delete / Restore: Delete / restore someone or something.
  • Emoji: Display a random DDLC emoji.
  • Poemgame: Play the poem minigame here on Discord.
  • Quote: Generate quotes from DDLC or search for your favourite.
  • Say: Make a Doki say something.

To use the bots, simply click the links at the top of the page to invite the bots to your Discord server.

If you have any issues, or just want to try the bots out, feel free to join the support server.
