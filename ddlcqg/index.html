Hi, welcome to the DDLC Quote Generator! - created by ohnoitsAvon

What is DDLCQG, you ask? DDLCQG is a Discord bot that allows you to interact with your favourite characters from DDLC.

DDLCQG started life as a quote generator but has since evolved into a fully interactive experience. You can do everything from giving the dokis a hug to playing a version of the poem minigame.

Below is a list of current features:

  • Actions: Hug, headpat, tickle, or boop a Doki.
  • Ask: Ask a Doki a yes or no question.
  • Attack: Let your inner yandere run wild.
  • Delete / Restore: Delete / restore someone or something.
  • Emoji: Display a random DDLC emoji.
  • Mentions: Say hello or goodbye to the bot.
  • Poemgame: Play the poem minigame here on Discord.
  • Quote: Generate quotes from DDLC or search for your favourite.
  • Say: Make a Doki say something.

To use DDLCQG simply click the Invite to Discord button at the top of the page.

If you have any issues, or just want to try DDLCQG out feel free to join the support server.
